About

The Cookie Company is a local bakery that makes their cookies from scratch. I wanted to target parents with school-age children, as New Strategists find this group spends 73% more on cookies than the average consumer. Custom hand lettering was used to reference the personal touch that they put into all of their products.

My Favorite Part:

I liked getting to work with a brand that had the opportunity to be fun and playful. Eating cookies should always be fun, so I thought their identity system should be fun, too.

What Challenged Me:

This logo is one that I initially made (very poorly) in the begining of my education. It was the first project I ever tried hand lettering and, to say the least, it was just not very good. I later decided to do a complete overhaul of the logo and start from scratch on the lettering. It was hard to toss out my previous logo idea but, in the end, I'm very happy with how the new version turned out.
