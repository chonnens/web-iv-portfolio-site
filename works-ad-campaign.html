About

The Lincoln Children’s Museum was in need of an ad campaign to encourage a visit from families who don’t currently have a membership. The target audience was parents age 25â€“35. The ads needed to focus on children’s creativity and imagination while bringing about an excitement and anticipation for what the museum has to offer.

My Favorite Part:

I loved getting to play with the kids as they dressed up for the photos. The fire station I worked with even took us on a ride in their firetruck after the shoot! I think I was as excited as the kid was

What Challenged Me:

Getting the concept and tagline just right took a while. I had a first draft that I thought worked well, but when I ran it by others they found it confusing. I was afraid I might have to scrap the whole idea, but I kept working with it until I found something easier for everyone to understand.
