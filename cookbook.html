About

I have a not-so-secret passion for cupcakes, so I decided to create my own cupcake cookbook. When looking at other cupcake books, I noticed most all of them were directed towards kids or people with kids. I decided to make a cookbook targeting women age 25-35 who love baking for themselves and their friends. They value taste and quality over colorful bells and whistles.

My Favorite Part:

This project was just a blast. I loved laying out the content, styling the typography, taking the photos, and, of course, eating the leftovers.

What Challenged Me:

This project involved my most complex photoshoot yet. There were dozens of little props and pieces that needed to come together as well as finding the correct lighting and composition in order for this to work out. I wanted a "Baker's Tip" on each page, so that added an extra element of planning while I took the photos. I am very please with how this turned out and how I grew as a photographer in the process.
