Kraut Restaurant & Catering

Skills Used

  • Ideation
  • Hand Lettering

About

This project was done pro-bono for a local developing restaurant and catering business named Kraut. They specialize in “Old World Food with New World Flavor,” such as bieroks and pierogies. They make their entire menu from scratch and by hand. Kraut needed an identity system that felt handmade, contemporary, and appetizing. I worked in collaboration with a fellow designer from start to finish.

My Favorite Part:

I loved getting to work with a real client for this project. We had in-person meetings, email conversations, and went through several revisions. It was awesome to make something that is actually going to be used, not just a concept. The owners of the business were great to work with and helped me gain a lot of experience.

What Challenged Me:

Coming up with the logo itself was the biggest struggle. We actually started this project in our third quarter of the program and presented a different version. They liked it, but wanted some changes. My partner and I decided to take on this project again for our final portfolio and give them the logo they really wanted. After a lot of sketches, brainstorming, and tweaking we finally came up with the mark we have now. The client was very pleased with the outcome.
