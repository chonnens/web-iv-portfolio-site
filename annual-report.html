Annual Report

Jones Soda Co.

Skills Used

  • Ideation
  • Layout

About

This annual report is targeted towards Jones Soda shareholders and enthusiasts. I chose the bright colors to reference some of their most iconic soda flavors. Stock photography was used to relate to their soda bottles in which every one features a black and white photo sent in by a fan.

My Favorite Part:

I had a lot of fun playing with the typography and color. I enjoyed taking their current brand style and fusing it with a bit of my own style to make something really compelling and energetic.

What Challenged Me:

I struggled at first with finding a balance between something conventional and something experimental. I think the layout ended up having elements of both which helps make it more dynamic and creates a good hierarchy.
