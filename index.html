A LITTLE ABOUT ME

Hello there! My name is Chelsea Honnens. I’m a Lincoln, Nebraska native and I love designing stuff. I started designing in high school, although to be honest I was mostly just doodling on my Myspace profile pictures. I later joined the Graphic Design Media Arts program at Southeast Community College and learned that knowing Photoshop does not, in fact, make you a graphic designer.

Studying design theory and practice from the past and present helped grow my skills to where I am now. I’m still learning and don’t have any plans to stop. I love creating work that is fun, tells a story, and brings the boring to life. In my spare time I enjoy being involved in the local design community through AIGA, spending time with family, and making spectacular cake pops.

I love working with type! Need someone to fine tune the nitty gritty details? Someone who knows that default leading is unacceptable? Then you’ve found your match!

From working with Wordpress to learning to code, my interest in web design keeps growing. In fact, I designed and coded this site myself with HTML, CSS, and a dash of Jquery.

I believe clear communication is the most important component of good design. I stay calm in the face of copywriting and would love to help craft a message unique to your brand.
