About

Nusse is a fictional brand that I created intended to target men age 25â€“35. I noticed an opportunity when I looked in the snack aisle and saw that almost all of the nuts were targeted to women or people on diets. This packaging is intended to reach men with a masculine styled packaging and by highlighting the bold flavor options.

My Favorite Part:

I liked getting to come up with my own company from scratch. Not very often do you get to start a project and choose, or even create, your client. I enjoyed coming up with a brand personality and style unique to them.

What Challenged Me:

The hardest part of this project was just getting started. I had several recent projects that I wasn't completely happy with and was afraid that this was just going to be another one. I decided to give it a shot anyways, and with a little inspiration ended up creating something that I was really happy with.
